Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sidibe Tutoring ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Mamadou Sidibe, collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website (sidibetuturing.com). By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use our contact form or schedule an appointment, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Age (to confirm eligibility for tutoring services)
  • Subject(s) of interest and current skill level
  • Preferred session type (online or in-person)
  • Any additional notes or messages you voluntarily submit

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

This website does not currently use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools beyond what may be inherently collected by your browser or hosting provider (e.g., server access logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Confirm and manage tutoring appointments
  • Communicate session details, schedule changes, or follow-ups
  • Improve the quality of tutoring services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

3. Children's Privacy

Our tutoring services are open to learners aged 5 to 65. For students under the age of 13 (or under the age of majority in your jurisdiction), we require that a parent or legal guardian provide consent and submit contact information on behalf of the student. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you share. Communication through the contact form is processed via email (ms8395074@gmail.com), which is secured by the email provider's standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — typically for the duration of our tutoring relationship and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for any ongoing communication

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ms8395074@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
